Sign up for Umbrella

How it works

Complete the form and we'll get back to you within a day or so to set up an initial call. This is so that we can start to understand your business and your needs. Once this is done, we'll set you up with an account on the Umbrella app and start your subscription. We'll also start work on your free custom dashboards and reports, based on what you've told us you need.

Once your account is set up, you'll be able to invite other people from your organisation to join Umbrella, and share your dashboards and reports with them.
